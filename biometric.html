How WAVE ID® Bio Works

Two Factors. One Motion.

Traditional MFA makes users carry a phone, wait for a push notification, or type a 6-digit code. WAVE ID® Bio collapses the entire authentication ceremony into a single physical gesture — badge on reader, finger on sensor. Two factors verified, session unlocked.

Step 1 — Present Credential The RFID reader verifies the badge — confirming the card's cryptographic identity against the enrolled record.
Step 2 — Fingerprint Match The Crossmatch optical sensor captures and matches the live fingerprint against the enrolled template stored on the reader.
Step 3 — Session Granted Both factors verified. The reader signals the host — workstation unlocks or door opens. Total elapsed time: under 2 seconds.
On-Sensor Privacy Fingerprint templates are enrolled and matched entirely on the reader hardware. Biometric data never leaves the device or touches a network.
