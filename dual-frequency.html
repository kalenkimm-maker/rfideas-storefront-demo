The Case for Dual-Frequency

Stop Buying Two Readers

Organizations that split prox and smart card readers across their estate pay twice for hardware, twice for support, and twice for training. Dual-frequency consolidates that — one SKU, one driver, one vendor.

Future-Proof Infrastructure Deploy once. When your organization upgrades from prox to smart cards, the reader stays. Only credentials change.
Reduced Migration Risk Credential transitions run in parallel — both formats active at once. No big-bang cutover. No user-facing downtime.
Single SKU, Simplified Procurement One part number covers every desk and door. Bulk purchasing is cleaner and support SLAs are simpler.
Works with Any Middleware rf IDEAS readers output standard data formats compatible with virtually every physical access control and SSO platform.
